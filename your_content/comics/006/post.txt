<h2>Author's Notes</h2>

Hewani came from many lands, from across the sea and beyond the Hazy Peaks, to forge an alliance and end the years of violence and discord that had plagued the many species of what would become Mothorial and its surrounding islands.

<h2>Transcript</h2>

NARRATOR: With the Elder's blessing, a monarchy was established to maintain order across the new queendom of Mothorial.<br>

NARRATOR: And so they ruled for many ages, and there was peace.<br>

NARRATOR: ...But it was not to last.
