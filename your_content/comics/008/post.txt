<h2>Author's Notes</h2>
(Be sure to click on the image to zoom in for a closer look! This will be the standard for all double pages in the future.)

The Battle of Merata remains the only time in Mothorian history that the monarchy called the citizenship to arms. Many young Hewani volunteered, eager to protect their land from the onslaught. It wasn't a long battle, but it was a bloody one.
