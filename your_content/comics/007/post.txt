<h2>Author's Notes</h2>

In Mothorial, on the day the seal of the Gate of Merata was broken (or so tradition holds), there is a Moment of Silence in remembrance. There is a second one not too long after.

<h2>Transcript</h2>

NARRATOR: The Younger grew jealous and impatient. She wished to return the world to the chaos deemed fit by the Old Mother<br>

NARRATOR: And so she intervened. The seal was broken.
