<h2>Author's Notes</h2>

The central star of the constellation "The Old Mother" is the North Star, used by hewani throughout the ages for navigation.

<h2>Transcript</h2>

NARRATOR: But nothing lasts forever.<br>

NARRATOR: And so the Old Mother brought forth her successor from her left horn.<br>

NARRATOR: To protect her kin from eternal solitude, she created a twin from her right horn.<br>

NARRATOR: From the Mother, two sisters: the Elder Twin, and the Younger Twin.<br>

NARRATOR: And thus the Old Mother passed into the Great Silence forever.
