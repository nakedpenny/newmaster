<h2>Author's Notes</h2>

The creation mythology of Mothorial is extremely culturally important to Mothorians. Most are told the tale of the Old Mother when they are very young.

<h2>Transcript</h2>

NARRATOR: In her wisdom, she brought forth, out of chaos, all that was.<br>

NARRATOR: Magic. Form. Birth. Life. Death.<br>

NARRATOR: Out of the Mother, into Mothorial.<br>

NARRATOR: And though chaos remained, the Old Mother was satisfied with her work.
